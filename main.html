Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissant - A buttery, flaky pastry that is a breakfast staple.
  • Escargot - A delicacy made of cooked snails, often served with garlic butter.
  • Macarons - Colorful, delicate, and delicious French confections.
  • French Onion Soup - A rich and flavorful soup topped with melted cheese.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
