Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking views from the top.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A stunning Gothic cathedral with beautiful stained glass windows.
  4. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe - A grand avenue with luxury shops leading to a historic monument.
  5. Seine River Cruise - A romantic boat ride offering picturesque views of Parisian landmarks.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissant - A buttery and flaky pastry, perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons - Delicate and colorful French cookies with delicious fillings.
  • Baguette - A classic French bread that pairs well with cheese.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes that can be enjoyed with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional vegetable dish bursting with flavor.
